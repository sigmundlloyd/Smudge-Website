This is a website all about Smudge the Cat

This is Smudge. He is a cat, specifically an Arabian Mau. He is white and ginger, and though he is sometimes crazy, he is a good cat.
One of Smudge's humans needed to make a website, so smudge suggested that a cat would be a good subject. Cats are pretty popular on the internet, and Smudge thinks he might become internet famous if this takes off. Take that,Grumpy Cat!
Now the website is done. Smudge hopes you will enjoy it. He says it could be better, but he is satisfied... for meow...
